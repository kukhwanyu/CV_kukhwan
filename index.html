소개 (About)

I studied aerodynamic and aeroacoustic analysis of rotary-wing aircraft during my Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University, and I currently work as a Senior Researcher at the Agency for Defense Development, developing unmanned aerial vehicles.

I analyze the aerodynamic and aeroacoustic characteristics of rotating machinery — rotors, propellers, and fans — and study designs that stay quiet without compromising performance, using deep-learning-based multidisciplinary design optimization (MDAO).

I have applied noise prediction and reduction techniques to real-world industry problems, including aircraft, submarine propellers, multirotor drones, earbud wind noise, and cooling tower fans.
